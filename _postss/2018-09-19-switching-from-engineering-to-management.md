## Notes

* Habits to get rid of:
  * Desire to do everything yourself
  * Desire to be know-it-all
  * Closing it up and working alone
  * Caring only about your part of the work
* Habits to improve:
  * Communication
  * Stress management
  * Decision making
  * Ability to adapt and learn new things
  * Accept that you will be wrong
  * Caring about the small things
* Benefits of management:
  * Meeting people
  * Influence on the direction of the company
  * Experience
